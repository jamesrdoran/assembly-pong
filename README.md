Pong game written in assembly

## how to play

### movement
left paddle: <br>
  'w' or 'W' - up <br>
  's' or 'S' - down <br>

right paddle: <br>
  'o' or 'O' - up <br>
  'l' or 'L' - down <br>

### restart the game
'r' or 'R' - restart the game
